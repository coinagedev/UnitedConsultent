Our Services:

Dotcom immigration offers every possible ‘feasibility’ to its clients in the form of its valuable services. We offer professional help and assistance at every stage of the process so you need not go elsewhere while processing or post approval of your visa. The following mentioned are a few of our services that we cater to each one of our clients:

Free Consultation

One of the foremost services we provide is FREE consultation regarding your eligibility or chances of success for your Canada PR visa. Our team not only checks on your immigration points but also give assistance in improving your points so you become eligible to apply.

You can claim your free consultation by calling us or even visiting our office. Our team is well-prepared to search and tell the best solution to you so as to cater to your immediate migration plans. You will get to know about all the countries offering immigration programs as well as all the needed assistance in taking decisions for picking your best fit. Afterwards, we also give application help.

Documentation Assistance

Documentation is perhaps one of the main stages in your Canada application cycle. It is vital to have all documents completed and included as any in-complete or falsely made document might prompt either defer or dismiss your visa chances. Since the country like Canada are highly specific about their migration related documentation, it is compulsory to present all the records and docs to prove if the given information of the candidate is right.

Not only the right documentation but documentation within the time window is important too. Any postponement in preparing or submitting documentation can lead to direct rejection of your application.

Dotcomimmigration not only helps you in collecting, sorting, making and arranging your docs in the right format, but also make sure to do submission within the prescribed time. We have a dedicated team of case officers to look after your documentations. They know the whole methodology of visa application and thus, they will share with you each and every detail regarding your documents.

IELTS training

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is a test to gauge the English capability of those who are seeking immigration in English prominent countries like Canada. IELTS test is one of the prickliest subjects in whole of the immigration process, as most of the candidates remain tensed about it.

At dotcomimmigration, we offer the best IELTS training so you can appear and get through your exams with flying colors! Our expert IELTS staff give assistance and learning material to all our clients. You can prepare all 4 modules of IELTS namely speaking, writing, reading and listening. IELTS score is evaluated on a band scale from 0-9 and the score needed is at least 7.

Pre-landing services

Dotcomimmigration offers all possible visa assistance that you may need at any point. Our team helps you in getting ready and presenting your visa application by offering the following pre-landing services:

  1. Pre-assessment & Counselling
  2. Educational Credential Assessment (ECA)
  3. Language test registration
  4. Application filling
  5. Submission of documents
  6. PNP filling
  7. Job assistance
  8. PCC and medical fitness certificates
  9. Other visa help as required

With us, your immigration plans are completely arranged! Our expert immigration consultants’ help with all the critical parts of PR visa application that guarantees successful approval! We further assist you in satisfy all the fundamental visa conditions and prerequisites by cautiously survey your credentials and check if anything is all together needed prior to applying. Our visa specialists will assist you with planning visa interviews as well.

Post Landing Services

Dotcomimmigration’s work doesn’t end with your visa approval. We even provide post-landing services in Canada so you can easily settle at a new place! Any arrangement that is needed from the moment you land in Canada is taken care by us in an absolute quick and professional way. We provide the following post-landing services:

  • Monetary arrangements/ new bank accounts in Canadian banks
  • Temporary accommodation, home hunt, purchase or lease
  • Applying for Social security card and other paper works in Canada related to health, banking, etc.
  • Applying for drivers’ license in Canada
  • Coordinating with Canadian employees for a new job
  • Hunting schools and applying for admissions for your kids
  • Enrol you in a English course to get the grasp of English spoken in your local area

Apart from these, you can ask us anything you need in Canada. We have an office in Ontario and our staff helps you handle all your re-settlement schedules.
